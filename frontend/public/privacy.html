(10) Firebase Cloud Messaging for Communications.

Flowinity Mobile (referred to as PrivateUploader, TPUvNATIVE, or TPU Kotlin), our application for Android devices, utilizes Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) to provide instant push notifications to recipients when a message in sent in Communications. FCM is a service provided by Google that enables the delivery of push notifications and other messages to mobile devices.

When you use Flowinity Mobile and log into your account, certain data may be sent to Google through FCM to enable the delivery of instant notifications. This data may include your device's unique identifier, your notification preferences, and information related to the notifications you receive such as message contents sent by yourself and others in group chats, or direct messages.

Please note that the data sent to Google through FCM is subject to Google Firebase's own privacy policy, this can be found here.

By using Flowinity Mobile and logging into your account, you acknowledge and consent to the transmission of data to Google for the purpose of providing an optimal experience whilst utilizing Flowinity's messaging features.
